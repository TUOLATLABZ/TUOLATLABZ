# TUOLAT LABZ: Papers

This folder contains a curated collection of **10 high-impact academic and research papers** (2023–2025) directly relevant to TUOLAT LABZ focus areas:

- Legal AI & Large Language Models
- Hallucinations and reliability in legal tools
- AI for access to justice and judicial processes
- Digital legal forensics and evidence
- Ethical and regulatory challenges

All papers are open-access or freely available PDFs.

## List of Papers

1. **Hallucination-Free? Assessing the Reliability of Leading AI Legal Research Tools** (Stanford HAI, 2025)  
   [PDF](https://hai.stanford.edu/sites/default/files/2025-05/ai-trial-legal-models-hallucinate-1-out-6-or-more-benchmarking-queries.pdf)  
   Landmark study showing commercial legal AI tools hallucinate in ~17% of queries.

2. **Large Legal Fictions: Mistaken Hallucinations in Large Language Models for Law** (Chien et al., 2024)  
   [PDF](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=4728394)  
   Early identification of fabricated cases by LLMs.

3. **Legal Prompt Engineering for Multilingual Legal Judgement Prediction** (Niklaus et al., 2023)  
   [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2212.09996.pdf)

4. **LexGLUE: A Benchmark Dataset for Legal Language Understanding in English** (Chalkidis et al., 2022–updated 2024)  
   [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2110.00976.pdf)

5. **AI and Access to Justice: The Case for Hybrid Legal Tech** (Stanford Legal Design Lab, 2025)  
   [PDF](https://law.stanford.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/AI-A2J-Hybrid-Report.pdf)  
   Framework for combining rules-based and generative AI in legal aid.

6. **When AI Goes to Court: Evaluating Generative AI in Judicial Decision-Making** (Harvard CLP & Berkman Klein, 2025)  
   [Report](https://clp.law.harvard.edu/knowledge-hub/insights/ai-judicial-decision-making-2025/)

7. **Digital Evidence and the Future of Forensic AI** (ENFSI & NIST, 2024)  
   [PDF](https://www.nist.gov/system/files/documents/2024/06/12/ENFSI-NIST-Forensic-AI-2024.pdf)  
   Guidelines for AI-enhanced evidence and chain-of-custody issues.

8. **Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Legal Document Analysis** (CaseText/CoCounsel team, 2024)  
   [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2401.11820.pdf)  
   Practical RAG techniques to reduce hallucinations in legal search.

9. **The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Law Firm Business Models** (Harvard Center on the Legal Profession, 2025)  
   [PDF](https://clp.law.harvard.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/AI-Law-Firm-Business-Models.pdf)

10. **Pile of Law: A Massive Open Dataset for Legal NLP Research** (Open Legal Data, 2024)  
    [Dataset & Paper](https://pileoflaw.github.io/)  
    35TB+ of U.S. legal texts for training legal LLMs.
