## Overview
In a criminal trial, AI-enhanced video (using consumer tools) was rejected as evidence because it altered original content and lacked acceptance in forensic community.

## Key Issues
- AI added/changed details, violating integrity.
- Frye standard: Not generally accepted by forensic experts.

## Lessons for Legal Tech
- Chain of custody critical for AI-processed evidence.
- Need transparent, calibrated forensic AI tools.
- Relevance to TUOLAT LABZ: Develop ethical digital forensics prototypes.
