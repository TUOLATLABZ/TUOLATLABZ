# Case Study: China's Intelligent Trial 1.0 – Hebei High Court AI System

## Overview
Hebei Provincial High Court deployed "Intelligent Trial 1.0" – an AI system for digitizing filings, classifying documents, matching parties, generating procedural docs, and assigning cases.

## Key Features
- Automates evidence scanning and law/case suggestion.
- Reduces workload for judges/staff.
- Coordinates multiple AI tasks in workflows.

## Outcomes
- Faster case resolution and reduced backlogs.
- Global example of "smart courts" scaling AI in judiciary.

## Lessons for Legal Tech
- Blockchain + AI for secure records.
- Challenges: Ensuring transparency in high-volume dockets.
- Relevance to TUOLAT LABZ: Inspire virtual courtroom prototypes.
