Post-Quantum Cryptography Migration (NIST & Global Standards)

As of December 2025, NIST has standardized key PQC algorithms to counter "harvest now, decrypt later" threats. Published in August 2024: FIPS 203 (ML-KEM, based on CRYSTALS-Kyber for key encapsulation), FIPS 204 (ML-DSA, based on CRYSTALS-Dilithium for signatures), FIPS 205 (SLH-DSA, based on SPHINCS+ for stateless hash-based signatures). FIPS 206 (FN-DSA, based on FALCON) is in development. In March 2025, NIST selected HQC (code-based KEM) as a diverse backup to ML-KEM for additional resilience.
